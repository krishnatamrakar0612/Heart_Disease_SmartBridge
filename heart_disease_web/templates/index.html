About The Project

From raw records to decision-ready health intelligence

Heart disease remains one of the leading global health risks. This project evaluates factors such as BMI, age, smoking behavior, diabetes, and physical activity to understand high-risk patterns.

The workflow combines data cleaning, SQL analysis, and Tableau visual storytelling in a Flask-based interface designed for exploration and learning.
