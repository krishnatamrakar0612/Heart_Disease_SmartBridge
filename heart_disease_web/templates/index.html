About the Project

Heart disease is one of the leading causes of death worldwide. This project analyzes different health factors such as BMI, smoking habits, physical activity, diabetes, and age to understand their impact on heart disease risk.

Using Tableau dashboards and a Flask web application, we present interactive visualizations that allow users to explore insights from the dataset.
